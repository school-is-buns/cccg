The process

How it works

Here's what to expect from submission to slab.

1

Pick a tier

Choose Economy, Pro, or Bulk based on your budget and timeline. Bulk requires at least 5 cards.

2

Fill out the form

Enter your name, email, card count, and tier. We'll reply with shipping instructions within 1 business day.

3

Pack and ship

Use top loaders inside a rigid bubble mailer. Don't send cards loose — we can't cover damage from bad packaging.

4

We grade

Each card is inspected for centering, corners, edges, and surface and assigned a grade from 1 to 10.

5

Get your slabs back

Cards are sealed and shipped back once your full order is complete.
